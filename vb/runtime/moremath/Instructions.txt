Instructions:
    To use MoreMath.dll simply add it to your project.  Here's how:

  1.  Place MoreMath.dll into your Windows\System Directory.
  2.  Click Project > References
  3.  Scroll down the list and find MoreMath
  4.  Double click MoreMath and press Apply.

That's it, your done.  Now just open your Object Manager, and find the MoreMath file in the list.

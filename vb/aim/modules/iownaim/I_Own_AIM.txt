Basic Codes:
Call AIM_Close (this will close your AIM)
Call AIM_Show_Ad (this will show the ad)
Call AIM_Hide_Ad (this will hide the ad)
Call Chat_Clear (this will clear the chat)
Call Chat_Link (address, txt)  (this will send a link to the chat
ChatSend (this will send txt to the chat)
Call Mass_IM (person, txt)  (this will send Im's to everyone you list)

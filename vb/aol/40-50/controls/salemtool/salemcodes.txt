A couple codes/hints about the .ocx...
to send chat simply type Salem1.ChatSend(chat)..
to get the last line of chat use Salem1.lastchatline..
to send mail is Salem1.Mail_Send(variables)...
ims are Salem1.IM_Send(var)..
ims on/off Salem1.ImsOn/Salem1.ImsOff
running the AI bot is Salem1.ChatWatch..
